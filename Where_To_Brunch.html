Where To Brunch

A small curated list of my top 5 brunch spots in Port Huron, MI.

Freighters Eatery & Taproom

Freighters Eatery & Taproom offers an extensive breakfast menu for all your "brunching" needs. From their Wakeup Doubletree Breakfast Buffet, to their specialty omelets and breakfast cocktails, there's plenty to choose from.

800 Harker Street, Port Huron, MI 48060
Tel: 810.941.6010

Kate's Downtown

Kate's Downtown cafe "cozy-core" menu offerings are perfect for brunch. You can dine in their cozy dining room or grab a bite to go. You can expect to find a variety of cold and hot sandwiches, like their Border Town Sammy (my favorite) or Avocado Toast - plus an awesome variety of drinks. Remember, brunch is served every Saturday from 9-2!

231 Huron Ave, Port Huron, MI 48060
Tel: 810.984.2233

Ted's Coney Island

Nothing spells breakfast more than a Coney Island! Ted's Coney Island certainly lives up to the hype with their classic diner fare. From their Famous Biscuits And Gravy to Ted's Famous Omelet, there's plenty to scratch that Sunday brunch itch.

618 24th St, Port Huron, MI 48060
Tel: 810.982.4866

Exquisite Corpse Coffee House

Exquisite Corpse Coffee House menu and atmosphere are every bit of the word exquisite. With their enchanting Victorian dining room, they offer a plethora of coffee roast options, as well as a variety of specialty drinks. Make sure to check out their delicious sandwiches, like The Jammin' Breakfast or The Millennial!

410 Water St, Port Huron, MI 48060
Tel: 810.989.5242

Raven Café

The Raven Café is a book lover's paradise! The cozy and quiet atmosphere are only enhanced by their equally cozy menu offerings. Everything from their Spinach, Bacon & Cheese Quiche to their build-your-own breakfast sandwiches, this place is a dream!

932 Military St #1, Port Huron, MI 48060
Tel: 810.984.4330
